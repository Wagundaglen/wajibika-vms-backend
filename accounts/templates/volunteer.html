Join Our Volunteer Community

Your time, skills, and passion can help transform communities. We welcome volunteers from all walks of life — no matter your background or experience, there’s a place for you here.

As a volunteer with Wajibika Initiative, you'll participate in impactful projects — from education outreach and healthcare drives to environmental clean-ups and youth mentorship programs. Our volunteers are the heart of our work, helping us create meaningful change in Nairobi and beyond.

Why Volunteer with Us?

  • ✔️ Make a tangible difference in your community.
  • ✔️ Gain new skills and valuable experience.
  • ✔️ Connect with like-minded, passionate individuals.
  • ✔️ Receive training and ongoing support from our team.
  • ✔️ Build your network and enhance your resume.
  • ✔️ Enjoy flexible volunteering opportunities to fit your schedule.

Types of Volunteer Roles

Education & Tutoring

Help children and youth improve their literacy, numeracy, and life skills through workshops and mentoring.

Healthcare Support

Assist in health awareness campaigns, medical camps, and distribution of essential supplies.

Environment & Clean-ups

Participate in community clean-ups, tree planting, and promoting sustainable practices.
